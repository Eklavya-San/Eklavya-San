Happy New Year 2023!

My wishes for you,
Great beginning for Jan,
Peace for Feb,
Happiness for March,
Cool for April,
Luck for May,
Success for June,
No Worries for July,
Gifts for August,
Love for September, Travel for October,
Joy for Nov,
Bliss for Dec.
Have a Lucky and Wonderful Year!

Let’s welcome the year which gives hope
Let’s welcome the year which gives happiness,
Let’s cherish each moment it beholds.
Happy New Year!

From Eklavya Ghodake
